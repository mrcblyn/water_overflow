Water Overflow Problem

The app will calculate and illustrate how much liters are in the inputted row and column glass when the inputted number of liters are poured into the topmost glass.

  • Row: Integer value for row of glass to check. Should be non-negative.
  • Column: Integer value for column of glass to check. Should be non-negative, and less than or equal to row.
  • Liters: Float value for number of liters to pour on to top glass. Should be non-negative.
